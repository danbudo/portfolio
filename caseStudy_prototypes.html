In early 2018 BDO introduced the Gertner led concept of “Mode 2” to the IT department. This bimodal evolution was realised through the hire of a team of business relationship managers, a solution designer, and an agile project manager. The new staff were to report to a newly appointed head of innovation.

The development team was seconded to this function with the purpose of assisting the creation of digital products that would increase productivity and profits output across the business.

My role broadened to include the generation of prototypes to test ideas out amongst ourselves, and against business requirements or expectation.
